Hi there, I'm ZHANG Qi (张琦)!

August 2022-Now, Assistant Professor (tenure-track) with Visual Computing Research Center (led by Prof. Hui Huang), College of Computer Science and Software Engineering, Shenzhen University (VCC, CSSE, SZU).

April 2022-July 2022, Distinguished Associate Researcher within Guangdong Laboratory of Artificial Intelligence and Digital Economy (SZ).

From May 2021-April 2022, I was a Post-doc researcher at VISAL, Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Antoni Chan. I earned my Ph.D. degree from the same lab in 2021.

My research interest includes Computer Vision, Machine Learning, Crowd Counting, Multi-Camera Surveillance and 3D Scene Understanding.
