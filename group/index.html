Dr. Qi ZHANG's Group

This is the website page for the intelligent multi-sensing research group (iMUSE). As a group of the Visual Computing Research Center, Shenzhen University (VCC, SZU, led by Prof. Hui Huang), currently, we focus on multi-camera surveillance, 3D reconstruction, 3D scene understanding, etc.

We are a new research group consisting of young researchers, and are dedicated to solving real-world problems, benefitting the people and the community, with our knowledge of computer vision, graphics, and artificial intelligence algorithms & systems.
