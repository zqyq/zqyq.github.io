iMUSE Group

This is the website page for the intelligent multi-sensing research group (iMUSE, where 'MUSE' means 'Inspiration'). We are a research group of researchers dedicated to solving real-world problems, benefiting the people and the community, and broadening the knowledge edge of computer vision, graphics, and artificial intelligence algorithms & systems (in Chinese: “立足真实问题，服务社群大众，拓宽知识边界”). Currently, we focus on 3D vision, 3D reconstruction, 3D scene understanding, crowd analysis and simulation, etc.
