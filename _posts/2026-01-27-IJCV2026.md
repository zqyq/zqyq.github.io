Our crowd simulation paper, “SynMVCrowd: A Large Synthetic Benchmark for Multi-view Crowd Counting and Localization”, has been accepted to IJCV! 

Congratulations to my students Daijie and Yunfei.

We constructed the largest benchmark for both multi-view crowd counting and Localization tasks.
