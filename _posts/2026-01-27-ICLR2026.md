Our crowd simulation paper, “EnvSocial-Diff: A Diffusion-Based Crowd Simulation Model with Environmental Conditioning and Individual-Group Interaction”, has been accepted to ICLR 2026! 

Congratulations to my student Bing Xue.

ICLR 2026 received a large number of valid submissions: close to 19000, and the overall acceptance rate is about 28%.
